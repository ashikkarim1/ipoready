- Post 1-2 per week, Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM user's local timezone
- Leave 3-4 days between posts for engagement cycle
- Optimal posting for 2-week campaign: Days 1, 4, 7, 10, 13, 16

### Hashtags (Add to each post)
```
#IPO #VentureCapital #FinTech #GoingPublic #RTO #TorontoTech #CapitalMarkets 
#StartupGrowth #FundingRound #EntrepreneurLife #PrivateEquity #TechLeadership
```

### Visual Optimization
- All slides are LinkedIn-optimized: 10.67:6 aspect ratio
- Download PPTX → Open in PowerPoint → Export each slide as PNG (1200x670px recommended)
- Or: Take screenshots of slides directly at 1200x670px for consistency

### Engagement Tips
- Respond to comments within 2 hours for first 24 hours
- Tag relevant VCs, CFO networks, and accounting firms in comments
- Pin the most-performing post (usually Slide 1 or 3) to profile
- Use LinkedIn's A/B test feature if possible (carousel posts vs. single images)

- Reach per post: 2,000-5,000 (based on audience size)
- Engagement rate: 3-8% (5-10 comments, reactions per 1,000 impressions)
- Click-through to trial: 2-5% of viewers
- Trial-to-paid conversion: 15-25%
