## Design Standards for All Posts

### Typography
- **Headlines:** 28-36pt Bold (varies by post to maintain visual hierarchy)
- **Body Text:** 13-16pt Regular (left-aligned)
- **Accent Text:** 14-16pt Bold or Italic (for emphasis)
- **Font Family:** Inter or similar modern sans-serif (LinkedIn-native look)

### Layout
- **Margin/Padding:** Consistent 0.5" margins on all sides
- **Section spacing:** 0.3-0.5" between content blocks
- **Text box padding:** 0.2-0.3" inside shapes
- **No decorative bars:** No header/footer bars, side ribbons, or colored stripes

### Visual Elements
- **Icons:** Large, visible, color-coded (red for errors, green for success, blue for info)
- **Colors:** Dominant color 60-70%, supporting tone 20-30%, accent 5-10%
- **Contrast:** High contrast between text and background (no light-on-light or dark-on-dark)
- **Avoid:** Placeholder text, AI-generated decorative elements, underlines under titles

### Engagement Hooks
1. **Post 1:** Problem identification + question for comments
2. **Post 2:** Statistics and proof of value
3. **Post 3:** Relatability through before/after
4. **Post 4:** Specific cautionary case study
5. **Post 5:** Competitive peer comparison
6. **Post 6:** Clear CTA with friction reduction
