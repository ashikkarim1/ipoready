## FAQ

**Q: Why is PACE™ not defensible?**  
A: It's a methodology, not technology. Competitors can reverse-engineer it in 6 weeks. Defensibility comes from data (historical IPO outcomes), not the framework itself.

**Q: When do we get defensible moat?**  
A: Month 6-8 (emerging), Month 12 (strong), Month 18 (fortress). Can't accelerate this — requires real customer data.

**Q: What if we don't invest in defensibility?**  
A: IPOReady becomes a commodity tool. Acquisition at 1-2x revenue ($2-6M) instead of 5-8x revenue ($100-200M). Cost: ~$94-198M in shareholder value.

**Q: Can Big 4 copy this faster?**  
A: They could build the tool faster (have budget + talent), but can't faster the data collection. Need 18+ months of real IPO outcomes. By then IPOReady has 2-year lead.

**Q: Should we patent this?**  
A: YES. File provisional patents for:
1. Context-aware regulatory rule adaptation
2. Automatic disclosure trigger detection (47 event types)
3. Multi-jurisdiction compliance engine
4. Decision audit trail + transparency framework

**Q: What if customers don't adopt decision intelligence?**  
A: Start with opt-in recommendations (low confidence 70-80%), improve over time. Build trust through transparency + accuracy tracking. By Month 12, recommendations will be >90% accurate.

**Q: Should we raise more capital?**  
A: Not required. Year 1: $1.4M investment funded by $2.5-6.8M revenue. Self-funded by Month 6-8. Can raise Series B in Month 12 at much higher valuation ($30-50M+).
