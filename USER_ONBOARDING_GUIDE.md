## Getting Started (5 min)

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Navigate to the Sign-Up Page**
   - Visit [IPOReady.com/register](https://app.ipoready.com/register)
   - You'll be guided through a 5-step registration wizard

2. **Step 1 — Select Your Market**
   - Choose **Canada** or **US** based on your target listing market
   - This determines your available exchanges and listing types

3. **Step 2 — Enter Company Information**
   - **Company Name**: Full legal name of your company
   - **Listing Type**: Choose from IPO, RTO, SPAC, Direct Listing, or Regulation A+
   - **Target Exchange**: Select your primary exchange
     - **Canada**: TSX, TSXV, CSE, Cboe Canada
     - **US**: NASDAQ, NYSE, OTC Markets
   - **Currency Preference**: USD or CAD (affects all financial displays)
   - **Language**: English or French (used throughout the platform)

4. **Step 3 — Tell Us About You**
   - **Your Full Name**: First and last name
   - **Your Role**: Select from the list
     - Founder/CEO
     - CFO/Finance Lead
     - Legal Counsel
     - Board Member
     - Other
   - **How Did You Hear About Us?** (optional): Referral source

5. **Step 4 — Create Your Login Credentials**
   - **Email Address**: Must be unique (you'll receive confirmation emails here)
   - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters (use a combination of letters, numbers, symbols)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter to verify
   - ✓ **Pro Tip**: Save your credentials in a password manager

6. **Step 5 — Review & Submit**
   - Confirm all information is correct
   - Click "Create Account"
   - You'll see a success message: "Account Created!"

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. **Check Your Inbox**
   - Look for an email from `verify@ipoready.com`
   - Subject: "Verify Your IPOReady Account"
   - Check spam/junk folder if not found

2. **Click the Verification Link**
   - The email contains a unique verification URL
   - Click the link to confirm your email address
   - Your account will be marked as verified instantly

3. **Account Review (24 hours)**
   - Your profile enters a 24-hour manual review process
   - Our team verifies the company information you provided
   - You'll receive an approval email when complete
   - During review, you can explore the platform but won't access core features

### Step 3: Complete Your Company Profile

Once approved, log in and complete these essential profile sections:

#### **Company Details**
- [ ] Company Legal Name (confirm, required)
- [ ] Headquarters Address (full address)
- [ ] Business Sector (e.g., SaaS, Biotech, FinTech)
- [ ] Business Description (100-500 words about what you do)
- [ ] Company Website
- [ ] LinkedIn Company Page

#### **IPO Target Details**
- [ ] Target Listing Date (estimated month/year)
- [ ] Estimated Public Float ($)
- [ ] Estimated Market Cap ($)
- [ ] Your Estimated Listing Valuation ($)
- [ ] Lead Underwriter (if selected)

#### **Leadership Team**
- [ ] CEO Name & Email
- [ ] CFO/Finance Lead Name & Email
- [ ] General Counsel Name & Email
- [ ] Board Chair Name & Email

**Time to complete**: 10-15 minutes  
**Where**: Account Settings → Company Profile

### PACE™ Scoring

**Q: What is the PACE™ Score?**  
A: **PACE** = **P**rogress, **A**ctivity, **C**apacity, **E**xecution.

Your PACE Score is an AI-powered prediction of your IPO readiness (0-100). It answers: "How close are you to listing day?"

Calculation:
- **Progress** (25%): % of tasks completed vs. timeline
- **Activity** (25%): Frequency of task updates & team engagement
- **Capacity** (25%): Team size & bandwidth based on your industry benchmarks
- **Execution** (25%): Historical velocity (are you speeding up or slowing down?)

**Q: What's a good PACE Score?**  
A: 
- 0-30: Very early stage (Pre-planning phase)
- 30-60: On track (Most companies during audit/legal)
- 60-85: Accelerating (Approaching filing)
- 85-100: Final stretch (Filed or nearly listed)

Benchmark: Average IPO company has PACE 52 when they file.

**Q: How often does my PACE Score update?**  
A: In real-time. As you mark tasks complete, PACE updates instantly.

**Q: Can I see my PACE Score history?**  
A: Yes. Dashboard → PACE Velocity Dashboard shows:
- PACE Score over time (line chart)
- Key drivers of your score
- Benchmark comparison vs. industry
- Predicted listing date based on current velocity
