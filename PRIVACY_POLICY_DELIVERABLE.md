### Section 7: Your Privacy Rights (Comprehensive)

#### GDPR Rights (EU/EEA - Article 15-22)
1. **Right of Access** (Article 15)
   - Request copy of personal data
   - Understand what data held and why
   - Response: 30 days (extendable 60 days)
   - Cost: Free unless manifestly unfounded

2. **Right to Rectification** (Article 16)
   - Correct inaccurate/incomplete data
   - Available through account settings
   - Manual correction available
   - No cost

3. **Right to Erasure** (Article 17)
   - Request deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
   - Exceptions: Legal obligation, contract necessity, fraud investigation
   - Response: 30 days
   - Process: Submit through account or email

4. **Right to Restrict Processing** (Article 18)
   - Limit how data is used
   - Prevents certain processing during review
   - Examples: Suspend marketing while disputing accuracy

5. **Right to Data Portability** (Article 20)
   - Receive data in structured, machine-readable format
   - Transfer to another service
   - Timeline: 30 days
   - Formats: CSV, JSON, XML available

6. **Right to Object** (Article 21)
   - Object to processing for marketing/profiling
   - Withdraw consent for any processing
   - We stop processing unless legal obligation exists

7. **Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making** (Article 22)
   - Right not to be subject to automated decisions
   - PACE assessments include human review available
   - Right to request explanation of automated scoring

8. **Right to Lodge a Complaint**
   - File complaint with Data Protection Authority
   - EU: Contact national DPA (CNIL, ICO, BfDI, etc.)
   - No requirement to pursue internal process first

#### CCPA Rights (California - §1798.100 et seq.)
1. **Right to Know** (§1798.100)
   - What personal information collected
   - Sources of data collection
   - Business purpose for collection
   - Recipients of data sharing
   - Response: 45 days (extendable 45 days)
   - Cost: Free (one per 12 months)

2. **Right to Delete** (§1798.105)
   - Request data deletion
   - Exceptions: Necessary for service, legal compliance, fraud prevention
   - Response: 45 days
   - Process: Submit through account settings

3. **Right to Correct** (§1798.100)
   - Correct inaccurate information
   - Available through account settings

4. **Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing** (§1798.120)
   - Opt out of "sale" of personal information
   - Opt out of "sharing" for cross-context advertising
   - **IPOReady:** We do NOT sell or share data
   - Right available for future compliance

5. **Right to Limit Use** (§1798.121)
   - Limit use of sensitive personal information
   - Restrict collection to necessary purposes
   - Sensitive info: SSN, financial accounts, location

6. **Right to Opt-Out of Automated Decision-Making** (§1798.100(v))
   - Opt out of profiling/automated decision-making
   - Request human review
   - PACE scores can be reviewed with analyst

7. **Right to Non-Discrimination** (§1798.125)
   - No denial of service for exercising rights
   - No price increase or different terms
   - Exception: Financial incentives (with consent)

8. **Shine the Light** (CA Civil Code §1798.83)
   - Request third parties we share data with
   - No cost, once per calendar year

#### CPRA Rights (California 2023+ - New/Enhanced)
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to delete consumer inferences
- Right to opt-out of automated decision-making
- Enhanced child protections (under 13)
- Enhanced sensitive personal information protections
- Right to appeal privacy rights decision denials

#### Multi-State Rights (Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Nevada)
Similar to CCPA including:
- Right to know, delete, correct, portability
- Right to opt-out of targeted advertising
- Right to opt-out of automated decision-making
- Right to appeal agency decisions
