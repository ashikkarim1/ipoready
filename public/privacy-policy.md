## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Residents)

You have the right to:

#### Right of Access (Article 15)
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Understand what data we hold and why
- **Response time:** Within 30 days
- **Cost:** Free, unless request is manifestly unfounded

#### Right to Rectification (Article 16)
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Update information through account settings
- Request manual correction of complex data
- **No cost**

#### Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Article 17)
- Request deletion of your personal data
- **Exceptions:** Legal obligation, contract necessity, fraud investigation
- **Process:** Submit request through account settings or contact us
- **Response time:** Within 30 days

#### Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
- Limit how we use your data
- Prevents certain processing while we review your request
- **Examples:** Suspend marketing while disputing accuracy

#### Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
- Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON, CSV)
- Transfer data to another service
- **Timeline:** 30 days
- **Format:** CSV, JSON, or XML available

#### Right to Object (Article 21)
- Object to processing for marketing, profiling, or legitimate interests
- Right to withdraw consent for any processing
- **Our response:** We will stop processing unless legal obligation exists

#### Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)
- You have the right not to be subject to automated decision-making
- PACE benchmark assessments include human review available upon request
- You can request explanation of any automated scoring

#### Right to Lodge a Complaint
- File complaint with your Data Protection Authority
- **EU:** Contact your national DPA (e.g., CNIL in France, ICO in UK)
- Contact information: https://edpb.ec.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en

### 7.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

You have the right to:

#### Right to Know (CCPA §1798.100)
- Request what personal information we collected
- Request sources of data collection
- Request business purpose for collection
- Request recipients of data sharing
- **Response time:** Within 45 days (extensible 45 days for complex requests)
- **Cost:** Free (one request per 12 months)

#### Right to Delete (CCPA §1798.105)
- Request deletion of personal information
- **Exceptions:** Necessary for service provision, legal compliance, fraud prevention
- **Process:** Submit deletion request through account settings
- **Response time:** Within 45 days

#### Right to Correct (CCPA §1798.100)
- Correct inaccurate personal information
- Available through account settings for most data

#### Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing (CCPA §1798.120)
- Opt-out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information
- "Sale" under CCPA includes data shared for cross-context behavioral advertising
- "Sharing" includes data disclosed for direct marketing
- **We do not sell personal information to unaffiliated third parties**
- **We do not share for cross-context advertising**

#### Right to Limit Use (CCPA §1798.121)
- Limit use of sensitive personal information
- Restrict collection to necessary purposes
- **Sensitive information:** Social security number, financial accounts, location data

#### Right to Opt-Out of Automated Decision-Making (CCPA §1798.100(v))
- Opt-out of profiling or automated decision-making
- Request human review of automated decisions
- PACE scores can be reviewed with human analyst upon request

#### Right to Non-Discrimination (CCPA §1798.125)
- We will not discriminate if you exercise your privacy rights
- No denial of service, price increase, or different terms
- Exception: Financial incentives (e.g., loyalty programs) must provide opt-in consent

#### Shine the Light (CA Civil Code §1798.83)
- Request third parties we share data with for direct marketing
- Available at no cost, once per calendar year

### 7.3 CPRA Changes (California Privacy Rights Act - Effective 2023)

Under CPRA, additional rights and protections:
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to delete consumer inferences
- Right to opt-out of automated decision-making
- Enhanced protections for children under 13
- Enhanced protection for sensitive personal information
- Right to appeal privacy rights decision denials

### 7.4 Other US State Rights

#### Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA)
Similar rights to CCPA including:
- Right to know, delete, correct, and data portability
- Right to opt-out of targeted advertising
- Right to opt-out of automated decision-making
- Right to appeal agency decisions

#### Nevada Privacy Law (SB 220)
- Right to opt-out of "sale" of data (sharing with third parties for monetary value)

## 20. Definitions

**"Personal Information"** or **"Personal Data"**: Information that identifies or relates to an individual, including name, email, IP address, device ID, transaction history, location data, and inferences about preferences.

**"Processing"**: Any operation performed on personal data (collection, use, storage, disclosure, deletion, etc.).

**"Service Provider"** or **"Processor"**: Third party that processes personal data on our behalf under our instructions and a Data Processing Agreement.

**"De-identified Data"**: Information that cannot reasonably identify an individual, stripped of identifiers and aggregated with other records.

**"Legitimate Interest"**: Our business interest that is balanced against your privacy rights (when no legal basis applies).

**"Consent"**: Freely given, specific, informed agreement to processing (opt-in; can be withdrawn).

**"Data Breach"**: Unauthorized access or disclosure of personal information.

**"Data Protection Authority" (DPA)**: Government agency responsible for enforcing privacy laws (e.g., CNIL in France, ICO in UK).

**"Sub-processor"**: Third party used by a service provider to process personal data (if different from primary processor).
