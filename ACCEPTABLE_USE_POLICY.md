# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** June 6, 2026

**Effective Date:** June 6, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") sets forth the rules and expectations for using the IPOReady platform (the "Service"). By accessing or using IPOReady, you agree to comply with this Policy. Violations may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account and access to the Service, as well as legal action where applicable.

This Policy applies to all users, including but not limited to: companies undergoing IPO preparation, advisors, legal counsel, board members, investors, and any third parties granted access to the Service.

---

## 2. Prohibited Activities

### 2.1 Illegal Activity

Users shall not use the Service to:

- Conduct, promote, or facilitate any illegal activity under applicable federal, state, provincial, or international law
- Violate securities laws, including but not limited to the Securities Act, Exchange Act, SOX, or equivalent regulations in other jurisdictions
- Violate antitrust, competition, or trade law
- Facilitate money laundering, fraud, or financial crime
- Engage in insider trading or securities manipulation
- Support or facilitate terrorism, sanctions violations, or embargoes
- Violate data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, etc.)
- Breach fiduciary duties or conflicts of interest laws
- Infringe intellectual property rights

**Enforcement:** IPOReady may report suspected illegal activity to law enforcement and relevant regulatory bodies without notice.

---

### 2.2 Automated Access and Scraping

Users shall not:

- Scrape, crawl, or systematically access the Service to extract data beyond their authorized scope
- Use automated tools, bots, spiders, or scripts to access, download, or copy Service content without explicit written permission
- Bypass rate limiting, authentication, or access controls
- Exceed documented API rate limits (where an API is provided)
- Download bulk user data, company profiles, or regulatory documents without authorization
- Use the Service as a data source for competitive products or third-party systems
- Attempt to reverse-engineer the Service architecture or algorithms

**Permitted Use:** Authorized integrations via documented APIs with approved rate limits are permitted. Users must obtain written consent for any large-scale data export or integration.

**Enforcement:**
- Immediate blocking of automated requests
- IP address bans for repeat violations
- Suspension or termination of account and API access
- Legal action for breach of contract and intellectual property infringement

---

### 2.3 Harassment, Threats, and Hate Speech

Users shall not use the Service to:

- Harass, intimidate, bully, or threaten other users, IPOReady staff, or third parties
- Send unsolicited insulting, defamatory, or demeaning messages
- Make threats of violence, property damage, or economic harm
- Engage in stalking, doxxing, or privacy invasion
- Use slurs, hate speech, or discriminatory language targeting protected classes (race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, national origin, etc.)
- Incite violence or discrimination against individuals or groups
- Publish or distribute private information about others without consent

**Enforcement:**
- Immediate suspension of account
- Removal of offending content
- Permanent termination for severe or repeated violations
- Cooperation with law enforcement for threats of violence

---

### 2.4 Malware, Viruses, and Harmful Code

Users shall not:

- Upload, distribute, or execute malware, viruses, trojans, worms, or other malicious code
- Create or spread ransomware, spyware, adware, or keyloggers
- Conduct denial-of-service (DoS) or distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks
- Exploit security vulnerabilities in the Service
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to IPOReady systems or user accounts
- Inject code into forms, uploads, or API calls intended to harm the Service or users
- Introduce vulnerabilities that compromise data integrity or confidentiality

**Enforcement:**
- Immediate account suspension and termination
- IP and domain bans
- Legal action for computer fraud and abuse
- Mandatory disclosure to affected users if data compromise occurs

---

### 2.5 Reverse Engineering and Unauthorized Access

Users shall not:

- Reverse-engineer, decompile, disassemble, or attempt to derive the source code of the Service
- Attempt to bypass authentication, encryption, or access controls
- Probe, scan, or test the Service infrastructure for vulnerabilities (without prior written authorization)
- Access accounts or data not belonging to the user
- Intercept or monitor traffic between users and IPOReady systems
- Attempt to gain unauthorized administrative access
- Circumvent license restrictions or usage limitations
- Copy, modify, or adapt the Service code or proprietary algorithms for unauthorized purposes

**Exception:** Authorized security researchers may participate in a responsible disclosure program with prior written agreement and strict confidentiality terms.

**Enforcement:**
- Immediate account termination
- Legal action for breach of contract, unauthorized computer access, and IP infringement
- Civil damages and injunctive relief

---

### 2.6 Spam and Bulk Communications

Users shall not:

- Send unsolicited bulk email (spam) through or using the Service
- Use the Service to distribute unsolicited marketing materials or advertisements
- Harvest email addresses or contact information for unauthorized mailing lists
- Conduct phishing campaigns or impersonate IPOReady or other entities
- Use the Service for multi-level marketing (MLM) or pyramid schemes
- Send repetitive or abusive messages to other users
- Abuse notification systems (e.g., alerts, emails) for spam purposes
- Use the platform to promote competing services without disclosure and approval

**Enforcement:**
- Account suspension pending review
- Removal of spam messages and associated content
- Permanent account termination for violations
- IP and domain blocking

---

### 2.7 Unauthorized Access to Accounts and Data

Users shall not:

- Share login credentials with unauthorized parties or use accounts belonging to others
- Attempt to access, use, or disclose data of other users or companies without authorization
- Exploit trust relationships to gain unauthorized data access
- Access confidential filings, board materials, or sensitive company data outside their authorized scope
- Use another user's API key, authentication token, or session cookie
- Attempt to escalate privileges or access administrative functions without authorization

**Enforcement:**
- Immediate password reset and account lockdown
- Notification to affected account owner and company
- Account suspension pending investigation
- Permanent termination for confirmed unauthorized access
- Legal action and mandatory breach reporting if sensitive data is compromised

---

### 2.8 Additional Prohibited Conduct

Users shall not:

- **Intellectual Property Violations:** Upload copyrighted, trademarked, or patented content without authorization or fair use rights
- **Misleading Information:** Provide false information to misrepresent company status, financial position, or regulatory standing
- **Fraud and Misrepresentation:** Impersonate other companies, executives, or advisors, or provide fraudulent documents
- **Unlawful Disclosure:** Violate confidentiality agreements, NDAs, or regulatory quiet periods
- **Regulatory Violations:** Use the Service to facilitate market manipulation, insider trading, or securities fraud
- **Social Engineering:** Attempt to trick IPOReady staff into revealing sensitive information
- **Network Abuse:** Consume excessive bandwidth, storage, or processing resources to degrade service quality for others
- **Third-Party Interference:** Interfere with third-party integrations, cloud storage providers, or regulatory data feeds

## 3. Enforcement Actions

### 3.1 Progressive Enforcement

IPOReady reserves the right to take proportionate enforcement actions, which may include:

#### **Level 1: Warning**
- Formal notice of policy violation
- Requirement to cease prohibited activity within specified timeframe
- Documented warning on account
- Applies to: Minor first-time violations, unintentional breaches

#### **Level 2: Suspension**
- Temporary (7-30 days) suspension of account access
- Inability to login or perform operations
- Restriction of specific features or integrations
- Applies to: Repeated violations, moderate abuse, policy disregard after warning

#### **Level 3: Permanent Termination**
- Immediate and permanent deletion of account access
- Data retention per legal requirements (see Section 3.3)
- Ineligibility for future service reinstatement
- Applies to: Severe violations, repeated offenses after suspension, illegal activity, malware distribution, harassment, threats

---

### 3.2 Immediate Termination (No Warning)

The following violations result in immediate, permanent account termination without prior warning:

- **Illegal Activity:** Any violation of law or regulation
- **Threats of Violence:** Credible threats to harm individuals or property
- **Hate Speech or Harassment:** Severe cases involving slurs, threats, or coordinated abuse
- **Malware or Hacking:** Uploading malware, attempting unauthorized access, or conducting attacks
- **Data Theft:** Unauthorized access to or exfiltration of user or company data
- **Fraud or Misrepresentation:** Material false information provided to facilitate illegal activity
- **Repeated Abuse:** Multiple violations after prior suspensions

---

### 3.3 Legal and Regulatory Reporting

IPOReady may:

- Report violations to law enforcement, regulatory bodies (SEC, FINRA, provincial securities regulators, etc.), and affected third parties
- Preserve evidence and cooperate with investigations without advance notice
- Comply with lawful government requests for user information
- Disclose violator information to other platforms and services to prevent abuse
- Notify affected users if their data has been compromised or unauthorized accessed
- Assist victims in civil or criminal proceedings

---

### 3.4 Data Handling After Termination

Upon account termination:

- **Company-Owned Data:** Retained for 30 days to allow retrieval; then deleted unless required by law
- **Backup/Archive Copies:** Deleted within 90 days per data retention policies
- **Evidence Preservation:** Data preserved indefinitely if required for legal disputes or investigations
- **Regulatory Filings:** Retained per securities law requirements (7-10 years for IPO-related documents)
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 2 years for security and compliance purposes

Users may request data export within 30 days of termination (at IPOReady's discretion and cost).

## 4. Responsible Disclosure and Security Research

### 4.1 Bug Bounty Program

IPOReady encourages responsible security research. Security researchers may participate in our bug bounty program:

- **Scope:** Vulnerabilities affecting Service security, data protection, or authentication
- **Exclusions:** Attacks requiring physical access, social engineering, denial-of-service
- **Process:** Report to `security@ipoready.com` with proof-of-concept (do not exploit)
- **Confidentiality:** Researchers must keep findings confidential until IPOReady provides disclosure timeline
- **Rewards:** Eligible researchers receive bounties ($500-$10,000+ depending on severity)

### 4.2 Responsible Disclosure Requirements

Researchers must:

- Not access, modify, or exfiltrate user data or company information
- Not conduct DoS attacks or tests on production systems
- Not disclose findings publicly until IPOReady provides written authorization
- Work within the scope of authorized testing only
- Maintain professional communication with the IPOReady security team

**Violation:** Unauthorized hacking or public disclosure voids bounty eligibility and may result in legal action.
