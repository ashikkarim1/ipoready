# Market Advantage Daily Engagement System
## Making IPO Planning a Living, Breathing Ritual

---

## 🎯 The Problem We're Solving

A static dashboard gets checked once, then ignored. Users need a **living dashboard** that:
- ✅ Updates in real-time (not stale data)
- ✅ Shows what CHANGED since yesterday (delta, not static)
- ✅ Alerts to urgent opportunities/threats
- ✅ Connects to their actual IPO timeline
- ✅ Benchmarks them vs. competitors and cohorts
- ✅ Drives daily action items

## 🔄 What Makes This "Living"

### Real-Time Updates
- Market sentiment changes: 5-min refresh
- Fed rate changes: Immediate (if announced)

- Your metrics: Daily at 11:59 PM (batch process)

### Change Highlighting
- Everything that changed in last 24h highlighted
- Trend indicators (⬆️ ⬇️ ➡️) on all metrics
- Color coding: Red (negative), Green (positive), Yellow (watch)
- New alerts sticky for 7 days

### Habit Formation
- Email at 9 AM (part of morning routine)
- 5-min dashboard check (quick dopamine hit)
- Action items clear and prioritized
- Progress visible (readiness score improves daily)

### Decision Support
- Every decision explained (why your valuation is $X)
- Peer context (how you compare)
- Market timing (when to file)
- Risk/reward (what could go wrong)
