## YOUR RIGHTS

### GDPR (EU/EEA) - 8 Rights
1. **Access** - Copy of your data (30 days)
2. **Rectify** - Correct inaccurate data
3. **Erase** - Delete your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
4. **Restrict** - Limit how we use data
5. **Portability** - Export data (CSV/JSON, 30 days)
6. **Object** - Opt out of processing/marketing
7. **Automated Decisions** - Opt out of profiling
8. **Complain** - File with Data Protection Authority

### CCPA (California) - 8 Rights
1. **Know** - What data we collect (45 days)
2. **Delete** - Request deletion (45 days)
3. **Correct** - Fix inaccurate data
4. **Opt-Out** - Stop data selling/sharing
5. **Limit Use** - Restrict sensitive info
6. **Non-Discrimination** - No penalty for rights
7. **Appeal** - Appeal request denials
8. **Opt-Out Automated Decisions** - Human review

### Multi-State (VA, CO, CT, UT, NV)
All CCPA rights apply in these states.
