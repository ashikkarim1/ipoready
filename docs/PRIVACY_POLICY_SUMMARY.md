## Key Policy Highlights

### Data Collection (Transparent & Justified)

**What We Collect:**
- Account data: name, email, company info, billing data
- Company data: legal structure, financials, board composition
- Documents: Files you upload (prospectuses, SEC filings, etc.)
- Usage data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps
- Communications: Support tickets, feedback, annotations
- Device data: IP address, browser type, device info

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- Full credit card numbers (Stripe handles)
- Passwords in plain text (bcrypt hashed)
- Biometric data
- Health information (unless disclosed in documents)
- Children's personal data (under 13)
- Precise geolocation
- Data for cross-context behavioral advertising

### Legal Basis (Clear Justification)

| Purpose | Legal Basis | Why |
|---------|------------|-----|
| Running the service | **Contract** | You agreed to Terms of Service |
| Payments & billing | **Contract + Legal** | Transaction necessity + tax law |
| Customer support | **Contract** | Part of service delivery |
| Security & fraud prevention | **Legitimate Interest** | Critical for platform safety |
| Product improvement | **Legitimate Interest** | Better service = win/win |
| Marketing (opt-in only) | **Consent** | You explicitly consent |
| Legal compliance | **Legal Obligation** | Required by law |

### Data Sharing (Limited & Controlled)

**We Share With:**
- AWS, Neon, Vercel: Infrastructure (no marketing use)
- Stripe: Payment processing (PCI-DSS certified)
- Resend, Twilio: Email/SMS delivery
- Google Analytics: Usage analytics (anonymized)
- Anthropic: Document analysis (AI compliance detection)

**We DON'T Share:**
- ❌ No "sale" to unaffiliated parties (CCPA compliant)
- ❌ No cross-context advertising (privacy-first)
- ❌ No behavior data for tracking
- ❌ No sharing with brokers/data brokers

**We DO Share When Legally Required:**
- Law enforcement (court order)
- SEC investigation (subpoena)
- Fraud prevention (necessity)
- Legal proceedings (disclosure required)

### User Rights (15+ Distinct Rights)

| Right | What Users Can Do | Timeline | How |
|-------|------------------|----------|-----|
| **Access** | Get copy of their data | 30 days (GDPR) / 45 days (CCPA) | Settings > Privacy |
| **Rectification** | Fix inaccurate data | 30 days | Settings > Privacy |
| **Erasure** | Delete their account | 30 days | Settings > Privacy |
| **Restriction** | Limit processing | 30 days | Email privacy team |
| **Portability** | Download data as CSV/JSON | 30 days | Settings > Privacy |
| **Objection** | Opt-out of processing | 30 days | Settings > Privacy |
| **Non-Automated Decisions** | Opt-out of PACE scoring | Immediate | Settings > Privacy |
| **Complaint** | Report to Data Protection Authority | N/A | DPA website |
| **Consent Withdrawal** | Revoke marketing consent | Immediate | Email unsubscribe |
| **Opt-Out Sale/Sharing** | Prevent data "sale" | Immediate | Settings > Privacy |
| **Non-Discrimination** | No penalty for requesting rights | Enforced | Built-in guarantee |
| **Correction** | Correct inaccurate data | 45 days | Settings > Privacy |
| **Deletion of Inferences** | Remove derived conclusions | 45 days | Email privacy team |
| **Appeal** | Challenge our decision | 45 days | Email privacy team |
| **Contact/Complaint** | Report violations | N/A | privacy@ipoready.com |

### Data Retention (Minimal, Justified)

| Data Type | Retention Period | Rationale |
|-----------|------------------|-----------|
| Account information | Active + 2 years | Business records & audit trail |
| Documents & files | Active + 7 years | SEC compliance & legal holds |
| Transaction records | 7 years | Tax law requirements (IRS) |
| Email logs | 1 year | Support history & compliance |
| Support tickets | 2 years | Legal disputes & service quality |
| Analytics data | 25 months | Google Analytics standard |
| Server logs | 90 days | Security & debugging |
| Backup copies | 30 days after deletion | Disaster recovery only |

**Automatic Deletion:** All data deleted upon account closure (with legal hold exceptions)

**Operational Controls:**

- ✅ Non-disclosure agreements (NDAs)
- ✅ Secure termination procedures
- ✅ 24/7 security monitoring
- ✅ Incident response plan
- ✅ Data Processing Agreements with all vendors

**Certifications:**
- SOC 2 Type II (scheduled)

- CCPA compliance (verified)

### Incident Response (Transparent & Timely)

**If Data Breach Occurs:**
1. **Immediate (1 hour):** Isolate systems, preserve evidence
2. **Within 24 hours:** Assess scope, identify affected users
3. **Within 3 days (GDPR):** Notify Data Protection Authority
4. **Within 30 days:** Notify affected users
   - What happened, what data exposed
   - Recommended protective actions
   - Credit monitoring (if financial data)
   - Support contact information

**Historical Incidents:** None to date (clean security record)
