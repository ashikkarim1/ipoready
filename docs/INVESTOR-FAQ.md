# Investor FAQ: What You Get & Why

## Quick Answers

### Q: What's the difference between IPOReady and Crunchbase?
**A:** 
- **Crunchbase**: Generic startup data. 99% won't IPO.
- **IPOReady**: Only IPO-bound companies. PACE™ score predicts IPO timing. All data pre-verified.

**Translation:** You see the 1% of startups that will actually exit big. No noise.

### Q: Why do I get PACE™ score?
**A:** PACE™ tells you exactly how IPO-ready a company is (0-100). You get:
- IPO timeline forecast
- What they still need to fix
- Early warning if something goes wrong

**Translation:** Instead of doing 6 months of diligence, you read one score.

### Q: Why do I get customer concentration data?
**A:** If a company has 1 customer = 60% of revenue:

- If customer leaves, deal falls apart
- This is a deal-killer you need to know NOW

**Translation:** We save you from bad deals upfront.
