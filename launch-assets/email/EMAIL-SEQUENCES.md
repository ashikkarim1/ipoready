## Email Strategy Overview

IPOReady's email sequences nurture free trial users toward conversion while delivering genuine value. The strategy mirrors our core positioning: **We augment your team. We don't replace them. We push the deal across the finish line.**

**Key principles:**
1. **Value-first messaging** — Every email delivers something useful, regardless of conversion
2. **Timeline-aware** — Emails respect the IPO journey; messaging changes based on stage
3. **Expertise positioning** — Showcase 100+ IPO completions as proof and guidance source
4. **Non-pushy CTA** — Upgrade CTAs are soft; focus on continued value and plan readiness
5. **Data-driven send times** — B2B weekday early morning (7am PT / 10am ET) for maximum engagement

---

```
TRIAL PHASE (Days 0-30)
```
