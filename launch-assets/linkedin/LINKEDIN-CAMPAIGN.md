## Notes for Team

### Why This Campaign Works
1. **Clear positioning:** We augment, we don't replace. We own data value, not AI.
2. **Risk-free hook:** 30 days free removes the final objection. Visitors become trials.
3. **Proof via data:** PACE score + 100+ IPO datapoints make the promise credible.
4. **Founder language:** No jargon. Every message speaks to actual pain points.
5. **Daily cadence:** 7 days of messaging keeps us top-of-mind through launch week.

### LinkedIn Algorithm Tips
- Carousel posts get 2-3x the engagement of single images
- Publish timing: 8-10 AM local time (US East Coast) = max reach
- Ask questions in comments to seed engagement
- Tag relevant accounts (Y Combinator, AngelList, legal tech orgs) if appropriate
- Use hashtags: #IPO #FundRaising #StartupLife (limit to 3-5)

### Post-Launch Follow-Up
- Day 30: Email users who haven't upgraded → "Your decision point"
- Day 45: "Stories from the field" — case study posts featuring real companies
- Day 60: "What we learned" — inaugural PACE benchmarking report
- Ongoing: "Tips & tricks" weekly content (auditor questions, cap table hacks, etc.)
