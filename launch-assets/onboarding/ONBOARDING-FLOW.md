# IPOReady Onboarding Flow
**30-Day Free Tier**

---

## PHILOSOPHY

**"Upload what you have. We'll help you get there."**

Users arrive with:
- Scattered documents
- Conflicting timelines
- Unclear task ownership
- No visibility into blockers

We meet them exactly there. No judgment. No "you should have this already."

Goal: By Day 3, they're using PACE score daily. By Day 14, they can't imagine closing without us.

## ONBOARDING FAILURE MODES (Fix Immediately)

🔴 **User logs in, sees empty dashboard, leaves**
→ Fix: Show sample data or guided upload wizard

🔴 **User uploads files, gets no feedback for 5 minutes**
→ Fix: Show real-time processing status

🔴 **User doesn't understand PACE score**
→ Fix: Add 30-second explainer video on dashboard

🔴 **No clear "next action" after upload**
→ Fix: Coach recommends top 3 tasks immediately

🔴 **Help system is slow or unhelpful**
→ Fix: Monitor help chat, retrain on common questions

---

## ONBOARDING GUARDRAILS

✅ **Every action gets feedback** (no silent processing)
✅ **Every screen has a "help" button** (always accessible)
✅ **No jargon on pages 1-7** (explain as if talking to a founder)
✅ **Mobile-responsive everywhere** (users will check on their phone)
✅ **Progress is always visible** ("You're 67% through onboarding")
