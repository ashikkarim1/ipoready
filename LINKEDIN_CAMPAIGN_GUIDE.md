# IPOReady LinkedIn Campaign — Implementation Guide

**Duration:** 6 posts over 2 weeks
**Target Audience:** CFOs, Finance Directors, Startup Leaders (IPO-bound companies)

## Campaign Analytics & Tracking

### Metrics to Monitor

**Engagement Metrics (Daily):**
- Impressions (target: 500-1,000 per post)
- Engagement rate % (target: 8-10% average across 6 posts)
- Comments (target: 20-40 per post)
- Shares (target: 10-20 per post)
- Saves (track as indicator of deep interest)

**Click Metrics:**
- Link clicks to demo (target: 30-50 from Posts 3, 4)
- Link clicks to learn more (target: 50-100 from Post 2)
- Trial signup clicks (target: 200+ from Post 6)
- Actual trial signups (target: 30-50 conversions)

**Conversation Metrics:**
- Comment sentiment: % positive, neutral, negative
- Question volume (% of comments that are questions)
- Brand mentions in replies
- Competitive mentions (Carta, Pulley, etc.)

### Attribution & Follow-Up

**Post → Lead Tracking:**
1. Add UTM params to all links:
   - `/demo?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=ipoready_pace&utm_content=post3_before_after`
   - `/trial?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=ipoready_pace&utm_content=post6_cta`

2. In Mixpanel/analytics, track:
   - Which post drove the trial signup
   - Time delay (same day? next day? week later?)
   - Conversion rate by post

3. Sales follow-up:
   - "I saw you clicked our post on milestone sequencing" (personal touch)
   - Route hot leads (questions, challenges) to sales

## Contingency & Optimization

### If Engagement Is Low (<5%):
- Increase reply frequency (respond to every comment within 1 hour)
- Add video clips to top comments (attention grabber)
- Cross-promote on other platforms (Twitter, Reddit communities)
- Ask: "What question would help most?" in comments

### If Posts Get Negative Comments:
- Respond with grace: "Valid point. We handle X with Y approach."
- Don't delete (shows confidence in your product)
- Route to sales for deeper conversation

### If Trial Signups Exceed Target:
- Increase onboarding resources immediately
- Prioritize early converters for case study conversion
- Extend trial by 7 days for overflow (good faith gesture)
- Use social proof: "Overwhelming response! 100+ companies in pilot now."

### If Trial Signups Miss Target:
- Day 4: Post 7 = "Top questions from the 6-post series" (re-engagement)
- Day 5: Email to LinkedIn profile visitors without signup (retargeting)
- Day 6: Post 8 = Customer testimonial video (social proof)
- Day 7: Paid promotion on top 3 posts (boost reach)
