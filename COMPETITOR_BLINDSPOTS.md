## Market Gap: The Coordination Chaos Problem

### The Real Problem (Unsolved By Competitors)

A typical 18-month pre-IPO journey involves:

1. **200+ tasks** across legal, accounting, finance, HR, governance, IR, ops
2. **15+ stakeholders** (CEO, CFO, General Counsel, CAO, Board Chair, Audit Committee, external advisors)
3. **Hundreds of documents** (cap table revisions, Board resolutions, legal opinions, financial statements, disclosure drafts)
4. **4 regulatory regimes** (SEC, exchange, state, Canadian provincial if TSX)
5. **8 distinct phases** (readiness → legal → disclosure → SEC review → roadshow → pricing → settlement → post-listing)

**Current solution**: Email, spreadsheets, Slack, disconnected tools
**Result**: 
- 40% of tasks missed or delayed

- Regulatory guidance scattered across 50+ tabs
- Teams confused about whose responsibility is what
- Costs balloon from $150K to $500K+ due to rework
