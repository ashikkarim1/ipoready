## Implementation Guide

### Step 1: Design in Canva
1. Create 6 new LinkedIn posts (1080x1350px format)
2. Use the design specs above for each post
3. Apply consistent brand colors:
   - Primary: Navy (#1E2761)
   - Secondary: Teal (#028090)
   - Accent: Gold (#F9E795)
   - Dark: Charcoal (#36454F)
   - Light: Cream (#F5F5F5)
4. Use professional fonts (Inter or Montserrat for headers, Roboto for body)
5. Include IPOReady logo watermark in bottom right

### Step 2: Optimize for LinkedIn
- LinkedIn recommends 1200x628px for optimal feed display
- Add 5-7 relevant hashtags per post
- Keep copy to 2,200 characters max
- Include 1-2 emojis for visual interest
- Bold key stats using **text** formatting

### Step 3: Schedule Posts
```
Week 1:
  Tuesday, June 3 @ 8:00 AM EST → POST 1 (PACE™ Introduction)
  Thursday, June 5 @ 8:00 AM EST → POST 2 (Cap Table Reality)

Week 3:
  Tuesday, June 17 @ 8:00 AM EST → POST 3 (Team Readiness)
  Thursday, June 19 @ 8:00 AM EST → POST 4 (8 Phases)

Week 5:
  Tuesday, July 1 @ 8:00 AM EST → POST 5 (Founding Team)
  Thursday, July 3 @ 8:00 AM EST → POST 6 (Early Access CTA)
```

### Step 4: Engagement Strategy
- **First 1 hour:** Respond to all comments personally (builds algorithm signal)
- **By hour 2:** React to top comments with thoughtful replies
- **By hour 4:** Share post to IPOReady company page AND your personal profile
- **By hour 8:** Monitor DMs for early access requests; respond within 2 hours

### Step 5: Measure Success

**Key Metrics to Track:**

## Brand Voice Guidelines

- **Tone:** Expert, empowering, not alarmist (warn about risks but position solutions confidently)
- **Audience:** CFO/corporate dev level → Use industry terminology but explain jargon
- **Structure:** Problem → Consequence → Solution → CTA
- **Emojis:** 1-3 max per post (use for emphasis, not decoration)
- **Hashtag strategy:** 5-7 per post, mix niche (#IPOReadiness, #PACE) with broad (#IPO, #StartupFinance)
