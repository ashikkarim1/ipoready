================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Issue: "This address is already registered"
→ The address may already have a profile created
→ Go to https://business.google.com and search for your business
→ Click "Claim this business" instead of creating new
→ Verify ownership

Issue: "Verification postcard didn't arrive"
→ Check spam/junk mail folder
→ Wait additional 2-3 days (delivery can take 5-7 business days)
→ If address is incorrect, update and request new postcard
→ Use "Instant verification" if available (via phone or email)

Issue: Profile not appearing on Google Maps
→ Google Business Profiles may take 2-4 weeks to appear on Maps
→ Verify profile is showing as "Published" (not draft)
→ Check Google Search Console for any indexing errors
→ Share profile URL: https://www.google.com/maps/place/[business-name]

Issue: Photos not uploading
→ Ensure images are JPG or PNG format
→ File size under 50MB
→ Images should be 800x800 pixels or larger
→ Try different browser or clear browser cache
